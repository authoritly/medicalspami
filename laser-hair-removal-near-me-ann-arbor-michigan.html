History of Ann Arbor, Michigan

Ann Arbor, Michigan has a storied history of innovation, resilience, and progressiveness that has shaped the city into the thriving cultural center it is today. With deep roots as a hub of education and intellectualism stemming from the University of Michigan's presence, Ann Arbor has evolved into a diverse, welcoming community that embraces people from all walks of life.

The University's early admission of women in 1870 and its staunch defense of affirmative action policies reflect its long-standing commitment to diversity, equity and inclusion that has helped foster Ann Arbor's culture of open-mindedness and inclusion.
